Launch Camp: Building a New Generation at Saleh Kamel Applied Technology School

As part of LK Hub’s vision to advance technical education and elevate young talents’ skills, the first student camp for the inaugural cohort of Saleh Kamel Applied Technology School for Ready-Made Garments and Textiles was launched in Tanta, Gharbia Governorate, for the 2024–2025 academic year.

Goals Beyond Traditional Education
The camp was not just an academic start, but a comprehensive educational experience aimed at strengthening students’ sense of belonging and developing their personal and leadership skills. Activities focused on positive interaction, emotional intelligence, creative problem-solving, and raising awareness of personal safety and protection policies.

Character Building & Life Skills
A group of experts and specialists participated, including:

Emotional Intelligence & Self-Awareness: Dr. Sherine delivered interactive sessions on emotional intelligence, helping students assess themselves and identify strengths.

First Aid: In cooperation with the Red Crescent, students received intensive practical training in emergency response and lifesaving skills.

Creative Thinking & Communication: Dr. Mohamed Hamed, capacity-building consultant, conducted workshops on creative thinking and presentation skills.

Academic & Technical Preparation
Students attended preparatory sessions including:

Introduction to technical competencies and specialized garment and textile knowledge.

Orientation to Arabic and German language curricula and cultural/scientific subjects such as mathematics and chemistry.

Practical fashion design workshops.

Inspirational Visits & Institutional Support
Dr. Lobna Khairy, CEO of LK Hub, followed up on activities. Dr. Zahra El-Sherbiny, fashion business consultant, also met students.

Team Spirit & Official Launch
Sports and group activities strengthened bonds. The camp concluded successfully, marking the official start of studies.

First Qualification Camp for Academic & Administrative Staff
Held 15–17 October 2024 to prepare for the 2024–2025 academic year. Focused on school vision, psychological and educational support, and operational readiness.

Visit of Gharbia Governor to the School
Major General Ashraf El-Gendy, Governor of Gharbia, and Mr. Nasser Hassan visited the school, praising it as the first of its kind nationwide in ready-made garments specialization.

Launch of Field Training for Students
Students began field training at Family Factory in El-Mahalla, with safe transportation and strict safety procedures.

Teacher Training at Saleh Kamel School
Five-day training on professional competency methodology led by Dr. Mohamed Osama and Dr. Gaber Galal under supervision of Dr. Mohamed Abdelmaksoud.

Evacuation Plan Drills
Practical emergency evacuation drills to ensure student safety.

Entrepreneurship Curriculum for Garments & Textiles
A specialized entrepreneurship curriculum designed with leading experts to prepare students for the labor market.

Students’ Visit to Faculty of Applied Arts – Tanta University
Students toured the Ready-Made Garments Department and attended lectures:

“Smart Clothing and Innovations in Garment Industry” by Dr. Seham Dawoud

“Pattern Magic” by Dr. Maha Ibrahim Araf
Included practical pattern drawing and draping on mannequins.

Visits to the Pyramids, Salah El-Din Citadel, and Safari Park.

Students visited LK-Hub pavilion. Met Dr. Amr Basila and Dr. Rasha Fathy. Federation of Egyptian Industries also hosted them.

Visit of Saleh Kamel Humanitarian Foundation

Participation in Ministry of Culture Event “Mahfouz in the Heart”
